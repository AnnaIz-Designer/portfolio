03 · Process

0 → 1 in steps.

01
User research — parcel anxiety

Users track parcels multiple times a day out of anxiety, not necessity. The app needed to reduce that loop — clear status, proactive notifications.

02
Information architecture

Mapped all user flows: add parcel, track status, manage archive, subscribe. Defined the tab structure and navigation hierarchy for both platforms.

03
Design system built in Figma

Built a token-based system from scratch — colors, spacing, typography, component states. Separate files for iOS (HIG) and Android (Material 3).

04
All screens — 2 platforms × 2 modes

Designed every screen in light and dark mode for both iOS and Android. Followed platform conventions while keeping visual language consistent.

05
Paywall and subscription UX

Designed the monetization flow — free trial toggle, annual vs weekly plans, paywall placement triggered by parcel discovery moment.
